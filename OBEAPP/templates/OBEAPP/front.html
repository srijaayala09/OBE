Who Are We?

We are the students of Department of Computer science and Engineering at Jahangirnagar University. We built this website so that any academic institution can easily measure the skill of a student efficiently.

MISSION

Exceed teachers’ and students’ expectations by going beyond software to provide best OBE Based Result Management System that transform exam marks(Both OBE and non OBE) into result, enabling them to analyze progress and lackings.

VISION

Our Vision is to build and operate OBE Based Result Management and launch it at market level. We believe that our system will enhance the effectiveness, efficiency and accuracy of a university’s result management. So we commit all the teachers and students to help in achieving their academic goals. We believe in work with the accuracy and best quality. We want to be known as the reliable, innovative and user friendly result management system provider in the Universities.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Currently it is in alpha-testing mode. We believe that we can achieve our missions and visions once we launch our system.
